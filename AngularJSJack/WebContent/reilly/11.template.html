ng-view: This is where the last piece of magic happens. In our controllers section, 
we defined our routes. As part of that definition, we denoted the URL for each route, 
the controller associated with the route, and a template. When AngularJS detects a route change, 
it loads the template, attaches the controller to it, and replaces the ngview with the contents of the template.
